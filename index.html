Case Study

Building Dot — The Full Story

Dot is a social discovery platform for college students. Here's the complete development journey.

The Problem

College students constantly ask "what should I do this weekend?" — existing platforms are too broad or too noisy. We built a word-of-mouth layer for campus life.

Development Timeline

Phase 1 — Research & Design

40+ user interviews across 3 campuses. Low-fi wireframes in Figma. Core loop: discover → experience → share.

Phase 2 — MVP Build

iOS app with SwiftUI, Node.js + Firebase backend. Real-time feed, location discovery, media upload pipeline.

Phase 3 — Beta Testing

200-student closed beta. Reduced time-to-first-post from 5 min to 45 sec. Fixed 60+ bugs from TestFlight.

Phase 4 — App Store Launch

Apple review approved first attempt. Campus marketing campaign. Organic word-of-mouth growth.

Phase 5 — Iteration

Community features, improved recommendations, analytics dashboard. Weekly updates from user feedback.

Tech Stack

Frontend: SwiftUI, MapKit, custom animations.
Backend: Node.js, Firebase Auth, Firestore, Cloud Storage.
Infra: Cloud Functions, APNs, Mixpanel.

Behind the Scenes

Making the AI Short Film

Production Pipeline

Step 1 — Script & Prompting

Scene descriptions optimized for AI video gen. Each prompt tested 5-10 times.

Step 2 — Generation

Runway Gen-3, Kling, Sora — each for different strengths: consistency, motion, photorealism.

Step 3 — Editing

DaVinci Resolve assembly, color grading, temporal smoothing, frame interpolation to 24fps.

Step 4 — Sound Design

AI music + manual sound design. Environmental audio to ground surreal visuals.

Step 5 — Final Polish

Color pass, film grain, lens effects. 4K export for YouTube.

Tools

Runway Gen-3, Kling 1.5, Sora, ComfyUI, DaVinci Resolve, Eleven Labs, Suno, After Effects.
